BYSA Food and Beverage Company Limited specializes in supplying high-quality natural rubber and technical rubber products for domestic and international industrial manufacturing needs. With a sustainable development orientation and a professional management system, BYSA continuously improves product quality and services to provide the best solutions for customers.

We supply a wide range of rubber products including RSS3, SVR 3L, SVR 10, SVR 20, and blended rubber products for various manufacturing industries. All products are strictly quality controlled to meet technical standards and the requirements of both domestic and international markets.

With a stable supply source and warehouse system, BYSA ensures fast delivery for both small and large orders with consistent quality and competitive pricing. We currently provide products to many partners, factories, and businesses in Vietnam as well as international markets.

Product quality is always BYSA’s top priority. The entire process from raw material inspection, production to packaging is carried out under strict quality control procedures to ensure stability and reliability for every shipment.

Throughout our operation, BYSA has always prioritized reputation and customer satisfaction. We aim to become a trusted long-term partner for businesses both domestically and internationally.
