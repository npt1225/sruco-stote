Standardized Natural Rubber Manufacturing Process at BYSA Rubber

Natural rubber is currently an essential raw material used in many industrial manufacturing sectors such as: tires, conveyor belts, rubber seals, shoe soles, technical components, and many other industrial products. To create products with stable quality, the manufacturing process requires synchronized investment in machinery systems, processing technology, and output quality control.

BYSA Rubber is currently applying modern production lines for RSS3, SVR 3L, SVR10, and SVR20 rubber products to meet the increasing demands of both domestic and international markets.

The production system at BYSA is fully invested with modern technologies that help:

  • Increase product consistency
  • Reduce impurities in raw materials
  • Improve finished product quality
  • Optimize production capacity
  • Meet international export standards

Modern Production Lines

BYSA Rubber invests in advanced machinery systems and technologies for rubber processing, ensuring that the entire production process is strictly controlled from raw material input to final finished products.

The production lines include:

  • 5-roll milling system for RSS3 products
  • Automatic milling and crumb cutting system for SVR3L
  • SVR10/20 processing line with multiple washing tanks and modern creper machines
  • Energy-saving GREENHOUSE drying house
  • High-tech vacuum steam drying system

Quality Control Process

All raw materials are carefully inspected and classified before entering production. Throughout the processing stages, workers and technical staff continuously monitor:

  • Raw material cleanliness
  • Product consistency
  • Color and elasticity
  • Technical specifications
  • Post-drying quality

Enhancing the Value of Vietnamese Rubber

With a sustainable development orientation, BYSA Rubber continuously improves production technology, enhances product quality, and optimizes operational processes to provide a stable supply of natural rubber for domestic and international customers.

BYSA products are currently widely used in many industrial sectors, meeting technical requirements and export standards effectively.

Conclusion

The modern natural rubber production process at BYSA Rubber ensures consistent quality, improves productivity, and effectively meets the demands of both domestic and international markets. The synchronized investment in machinery systems, drying technology, and quality control is contributing to enhancing the value of Vietnam’s rubber industry in the global market.
