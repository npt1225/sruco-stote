SVR 3L

SVR3L Natural Rubber Meets International Export Standards

SVR3L natural rubber is one of the technical rubber grades widely used in many industrial manufacturing sectors today. With high purity, bright color, and good stability, SVR3L is becoming a preferred material for both domestic and international enterprises.

To meet technical standards and export requirements, many factories have invested in modern production lines to improve product quality, optimize capacity, and ensure consistency across each production batch.

Key advantages of SVR3L natural rubber:

  • Bright and uniform color
  • Low impurity content, high purity
  • Good elasticity and flexibility
  • Meets export technical standards
  • Stable quality across batches

Modern SVR3L Production Line

Modern SVR3L production lines are typically equipped with automatic crumb cutting and milling systems with high capacity, enabling fast and efficient raw material processing.

The production process includes:

  • Coagulation of latex
  • Automatic milling and pulling system
  • Standard crumb rubber cutting
  • Draining and drying process
  • Quality inspection and packaging

Vacuum Steam Drying Technology

One of the key highlights of modern SVR3L rubber is the application of vacuum steam drying technology. This technology helps improve product appearance, stabilize quality, and reduce direct heat impact on rubber materials.

Compared to traditional oil drying methods, vacuum steam drying significantly improves technical indicators such as:

  • Po
  • PRI
  • Nt
  • Dirt
  • Ash
  • Colour

Applications of SVR3L Natural Rubber

Thanks to its stable quality and high technical performance, SVR3L is widely used in many industrial sectors:

  • Tire manufacturing industry
  • Gasket and sealing production
  • Mechanical engineering industry
  • Conveyor belts and drive belts
  • Technical rubber industry
